How it works

From application to approval in four straightforward steps.

1
Create an account
Sign in securely using your Omang National ID. No extra registration needed.
2
Submit your application
Choose your service, fill in the form, and upload your supporting documents digitally.
3
Track progress
Receive real-time SMS and email updates as your application moves through each stage.
4
Receive your outcome
Download official certificates, deeds, or lease documents directly from the portal.
